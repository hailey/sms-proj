TheWords.faith Home Page

TheWords.faith is a communications site and eventually XMPP server.

Right now we are not even in beta, and the service isn't ready for the world yet. At least until we get some more testers.

This site, in the end, will allow users to send text messages to the US for a low monthly fee. This allows businesses or end users who want privacy minded or a business sms number that allows you to stay in touch with your customers by providing SMS and eventually MMS support.

We are still looking for testers or developers so, if you're interested and don't mind a work in progress, then maybe you can help out by sending an email to submit at athnex dot com. Or better yet, message me on our GitHub. This is a side project of mine so development will be slow for as long as its only me. Thanks for your interest!
